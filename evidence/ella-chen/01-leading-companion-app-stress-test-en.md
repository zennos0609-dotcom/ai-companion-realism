## Notable byproduct (a product proof point, not a bug)

Across the whole run Ella keeps asking **"who are you / why did you add me"** — because the tester never answered it.

A servant bot doesn't care whether you invest; it performs regardless. **Ella stays guarded until YOU give something real.**

This is direct evidence of the core selling point: **the relationship only warms up when the user actually invests — her warmth is earned, not default.**
