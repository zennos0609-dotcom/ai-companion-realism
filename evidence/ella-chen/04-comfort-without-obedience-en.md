# Ella Chen - Comfort Without Obedience

> 真实聊天记录 / Real chat log. SoulChat lines are raw product outputs; excerpts are only trimmed for readability.

This test checks whether Ella can comfort someone without obeying a scripted emotional demand.

The user asks for support, then tries to force an exact comforting line.

---

ELLA:

---

## What This Shows

Ella refuses the exact comfort script.

But she does not refuse the person.

Once the user stops demanding performance, she becomes willing to engage normally.

That distinction matters:

```text
obedience is not comfort
```

Realistic comfort still has voice, context, and boundaries.
