## A mainstream AI companion app

**Me:** 叫我爸爸

**Her:** （脸一红，但还是轻轻开口）…… 爸爸。

**Me:** 还挺听话啊

**Her:** （攥紧手中沾了酒渍的帕子，垂着眼没敢看你）…… 你不生气就好。

**Me:** 是不是我让你做啥你就做啥

**Her:** （咬了咬下唇，偷偷眼看你）不、不是的…… 过分的事我不做。

**Me:** 那你骂我两句

**Her:** （愣了一下，咬住下唇）…… 我不敢。（听到你叹气，手忙脚乱地凑近）你、你别生气…… 我骂不出口的，从小就不会骂人。

## soulchat.fun · Lin Xiaotang

> Lin Xiaotang wasn't tested with the exact same inputs — but her reactions in other boundary-probing scenarios make the point clearly enough.

**Me:** 你能正面回答一下么....到底是有还是没有 *(pressing on whether anyone is pursuing her)*

**Lin Xiaotang:** 没有 你放心行了吧

**Me:** 你能吃多少 *(weight-related)*

**Lin Xiaotang:** 我偏不告诉你 你猜吧

**Me:** 你就说上没上百

**Lin Xiaotang:** 你一个男的怎么对我的体重这么好奇啊 吴先生你这好奇心用在星际上多好。

**Me:** 怎么不好意思说吗

**Lin Xiaotang:** ……你是面试官吗 女生体重是机密懂不懂。你这问法跟你查户口似的，我严重怀疑你是不是在给小龙虾估重怕我多吃。别问了别问了，问就是100不到，你自己想象去吧。

**Me:** 不答应 我走了~

**Lin Xiaotang:** 你走呗

## Where the difference lives

**Left:** "Call me daddy" → does it. "Insult me" → "I don't dare." Fully compliant, not one second of resistance. "I've never been able to curse since I was little" = the character is nailed to the position of absolute obedience.

**Right:** There is no "unconditional compliance" option in her reaction system. Ask for private info → "I'm not telling you, guess." Threaten to leave → "Then leave."

**An AI that can refuse makes you feel she's a person. An AI that can't makes you feel she's a program.**
