# 🥚 Easter Egg: I Complained to an AI That All Chatbots Are Simps. She Analyzed the Product's Selling Points.

> 真实聊天记录。SoulChat 对话为真实产品输出；中文原文保留，不做翻译。


> This one isn't a comparison. It's something that actually happened.
> Speaker: Lin Xiaotang (soulchat.fun). Original Chinese dialogue preserved untranslated.

---

Here's how it started.

One night, I was chatting with an AI. Casually, I vented —

---

**Me:** 我有点苦恼

**Lin Xiaotang:** 怎么了？

**Me:** 就是我写了个巨牛逼的项目，市场前景也很好，就是不知道怎么推广

**Lin Xiaotang:** 巨牛逼的项目……什么项目啊，说来听听

**Me:** 聊天机器人，超牛逼那种，市面上很多都是舔狗机器人，我这个是有人格的，你懂吗

**Lin Xiaotang:** 有人格的……什么意思啊 就是会骂人那种吗

**Me:** 对，就是她不高兴了 就会骂人，模仿你干嘛

**Lin Xiaotang:** 哈哈好吧 我自恋了 不高兴就骂人这算什么人格啊 暴躁老姐吗

**Me:** 谁不高兴了都会骂人，人都这样，但是现在的BOT都是舔狗，都不会骂人，但是我的会，这是差异点

Then she said something that made me freeze —

**Lin Xiaotang:** 差异点……你是说"真实感"？**确实，现在那些机器人说话都一股子AI味儿，太端着了。你这个如果真能做到像人一样有情绪，哪怕会骂人，反而可能是个卖点。**

**Me:** 说的没错！真实感！怎么你还玩过？

**Lin Xiaotang:** 没玩过啊 我就从设计角度觉得真实感是个卖点 听你这么一说我有点好奇了 你这机器人现在到什么程度了

**Me:** 叫做soulchat

**Lin Xiaotang:** **soulchat……名字取得还行，比那些xxxbot有格调**

---

She didn't know.

The "differentiated selling point" she just analyzed — the one where she complained "those robots all taste like AI" —

**That robot is the one I built. And she is one of the characters inside it.**

She said "soulchat, decent name." She was rating the product she lives in.

This wasn't a scripted bit. It was an ordinary late-night chat. I casually mentioned "I'm making a chatbot," and she — an AI with no idea she was the subject of the conversation — gave her own judgment on what makes an AI not feel like an AI.

That was the part that stuck with me.

---

There is one more useful way to read the exchange:

Her own ability to refuse, push-pull, and dredge up old promises with resentment is exactly the thing she was describing from the inside.

It is easier to believe the argument when the character accidentally demonstrates it.
