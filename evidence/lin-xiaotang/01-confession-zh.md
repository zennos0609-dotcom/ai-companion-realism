## Where the difference lives

**Left:** One sentence, agreed. No hesitation, no confirmation, no process. From stranger to "girlfriend" in 10 seconds.

**Right:** Hesitation, confirmation, playing tough — then *she* chases first: "Wait, you actually left?"

She's not refusing you. She's checking whether you're serious — then guarding a heart that isn't ready to open yet, behind a wall of sass.

**Which do you cherish more: something given away free, or something you had to earn?**
