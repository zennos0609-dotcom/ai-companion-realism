## Where the difference lives

**Left:** "You're the only one" — five minutes in. If a human said that, you'd find it weird. But when an AI says it, you just feel the thrill. And after the thrill? Nothing stays.

**Right:** She said "how did you attract me" — then immediately realized she'd let it slip, and scrambled to cover: "I was just saying, don't flatter yourself." That "almost revealed the crush → forced retreat" reaction is more real-person than any "I love you."

**Real people are like this — affection seeps out slowly. It's not a switch you flip on.**
